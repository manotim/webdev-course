CHUN HEI KIM

Interior Designer based in Seoul

Creating dynamic commercial spaces and tranquil residential homes are my twin passions.

ABOUT ME

To me, spaces are exciting blank canvases, full of possibility. They offer a chance to express the tastes and preferences of their inhabitants, and influence the mood of customers or vibe of an establishment.

In short, spaces are living entities, and it gives me great pleasure to use my 10 years of interior design experience to help them meet their fullest potential.
